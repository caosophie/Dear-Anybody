about the project.

The 'Dear Anybody' campaign was created little over a year ago.

The idea came when Patrick and I were sharing about the pressure we face as students. We noticed that while many of our peers has experienced the similar feelings of anxiety, many were hesitant to seek help due to the stigma surrounding mental health and mental illness.

That's when we thought of creating a place where students --people-- could share about their insecurities, mental health, passions, accomplishments, and so much more. The purpose of such platform was to open the door to honest conversations about mental health and to raise awareness about the cause.

And so, camera in hand, we started filming students across campus. As we posted our first video, we were surprised at the number people who related to the story and who were willing to share theirs. I've heard many beautiful, touching and honest things during our filming sessions. I really hope you can find yourself through the posted stories, and that you'll enjoy them as much as I did.

Sophie

Dear Anybody is centered around story-telling.

We believe in destigmatizing mental health, one story at the time.
